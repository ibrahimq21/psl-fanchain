---

## Slide 2: What is PSL FanChain?
PSL FanChain is a Web3 fan engagement platform for PSL cricket fans.

**Core Features:**
- QR Code Stadium Check-in
- NFT Attendance Certificates
- Reward Points System
- Influencer Campaign Dashboard

---

## Slide 3: Problem It Solves

**Fan Pain Points:**
- No proof of match attendance
- Limited engagement with teams
- No rewards for loyal fans
- Disconnected online/offline experience

**Our Solution:**
- Blockchain-verified attendance
- NFT collectibles for each match
- Points-based rewards
- Unified fan experience

## Slide 5: Features

1. **QR Check-in** - Scan stadium QR to verify attendance
2. **Geo-verification** - Confirms user is at stadium location
3. **NFT Minting** - Auto-mints attendance NFT after check-in
4. **EIP-712 Security** - Server-signed proofs prevent fake minting
5. **Rewards** - Earn points for each match attended
6. **Influencer Dashboard** - Create campaigns & track engagement
